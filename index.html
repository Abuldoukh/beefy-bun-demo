Our Story

A better burger, obsessed over.

Beefy Bun started with a simple conviction: a smash burger should be an event, not an afterthought. We press fresh, never-frozen beef onto a screaming-hot flat top until the edges go golden and crisp — that Maillard crust is where the flavor lives.

Everything else is built to match. Buns toasted in beef fat. Sauces made in-house every morning. Pickles we brine ourselves. Nothing is here by accident — every element earns its place.
