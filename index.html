Our Story

Fire, meat,
and a little obsession.

Beefy Bun started with one belief: a smash burger should be unforgettable. Every patty is hand-pressed, every bun toasted to order, every sauce built from scratch. No short cuts, no frozen inventory — just real food made with real intent.

We're not trying to be everything to everyone. We serve great burgers in a room that feels like home, and we stay open late because hunger doesn't punch a clock.
